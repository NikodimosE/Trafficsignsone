Speed Limit Signs

Remember that in areas of street lighting (other than on motorways) a 30-mph limit applies unless another limit is specifically signed.

A larger sign indicates the start of a speed limit. Smaller repeater signs act as reminders. Repeater signs for a 30 mph limit are used only on roads with no street lighting. Repeater signs for the national speed limit are used only on roads with street lighting (other than UK motorways)
